Rajshri Hotel    

Address: Door No 20/68, Railway Station Road, SKD Nagar, Adoni, Andhra Pradesh 518301 (near HBG Nagar)

Description: A modest 2-star accommodation strategically located close to the railway station, Rajshri Hotel offers convenient access for transit travelers. The property features both AC and non-AC rooms with spacious accommodations and clean bathrooms. The hotel is renowned for its purely vegetarian restaurant that serves delicious Andhra cuisine, particularly praised for dishes like Veg Kolhapuri. Guests appreciate the friendly staff, quick service, and the restaurant's popularity among locals, though the property lacks elevator facilities. The hotel maintains a homey atmosphere with basic amenities suitable for budget-conscious travelers.

Key Features:

  • Pure vegetarian restaurant (highly rated for food quality)
  • Both AC and non-AC room options
  • Spacious rooms and bathrooms
  • Close proximity to railway station (main road location)
  • 24-hour dining service
  • No elevator (multi-story building)
  • Limited parking facilities
