Town Amenities

Explore the facilities and services available in Harmony Creek.

Parks

Greenwood Park

A large park with walking trails, picnic areas, and a playground.

Willow Creek Park

A smaller, more secluded park with a creek and natural beauty.

Maplewood Park

A park with sports fields, a community garden, and a dog park.

Community Centers

Harmony Creek Community Center

Offers a variety of programs and activities for all ages, including fitness classes, arts and crafts, and senior programs.

Northwood Community Center

Focuses on youth programs and sports leagues, with a gymnasium and meeting rooms.

Libraries

Harmony Creek Public Library

The main library branch with a large collection of books, computers, and study rooms.

Southwood Branch Library

A smaller branch library with a focus on children's and young adult literature.

Transportation

Town Bus Stops

Conveniently located bus stops throughout the town for easy public transit.

Harmony Creek Train Station

Connecting our town to neighboring cities with regular train services.

Entertainment

Creekview Movie Theater

Showing the latest blockbusters and independent films in a comfortable setting.
