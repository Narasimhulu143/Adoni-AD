ADONI

Welcome to Adoni

A city rich in history, often referred to as the "second Mumbai" or the "grain market of the south" due to its significant cotton and general trade industries. Located in the Kurnool district of Andhra Pradesh, Adoni offers a charming blend of ancient heritage and vibrant local culture.

Key Highlights for Visitors

  • Historical Significance: Adoni was a prominent fort town and a military stronghold. The remnants of the majestic Adoni Fort offer a glimpse into its storied past.
  • Pilgrimage Centers: The city and its surroundings are home to several important religious sites, including the revered Ranamandala Anjaneya Swamy Temple on a hilltop.
  • Natural Beauty: Visitors can explore the serene Ramjala Lake and the surrounding hills, which offer a peaceful escape and picturesque views.
  • Economic Hub: Adoni is a major trading center, famous for its cotton products, textiles, and groundnut oil production.
  • Culture & Cuisine: The local culture is a blend of Andhra and Karnataka traditions. Food lovers can indulge in local delicacies like Andhra Biryani and Pulihora.

Adoni is a less crowded destination, providing a relaxed atmosphere for those looking to explore a hidden gem in South India.
